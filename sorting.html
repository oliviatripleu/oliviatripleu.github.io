# Sorts Analysis
## Bubble sort:
- Stable
- Side-by-side swapping
- Time complexity:
- Worse case (reverse sorted): O(n^2)
- Best case (sorted): O(n)
- Adaptable
## Insertion sort
- Stable
- Side-by-side swapping
- Time complexity:
- Worse case (reverse sorted): O(n^2)
- Best case (sorted): O(n)
- Adaptable
## Selection sort
- Unstable
- Swapping jumps around
- Time complexity:
- Cost is the same regardless
- Reverse sorted: O(n^2)
- Sorted: O(n^2)
- Not adaptable
## Merge sort
- Stable
- Side-by-side swapping
- Time complexity:
- Worse case (reverse sorted): O(n * log(n))
- Best case (sorted): O(n * log(n))
- Not adaptable
## Naive quicksort
- Unstable
- Swapping jumps around
- Time complexity:
- Worse case (every choice of pivot gives one of the highest or lowest values): O(n^2)
- Best case (every choice of pivot gives two equal halves): O(n * log(n))
- Adaptable
## Median-of-three quicksort
- Unstable
- Swapping jumps around
- Time complexity:
- Average: O(n * log(n))
- Adaptable
## Randomised quicksort
- Unstable
- Swapping jumps around
- Time complexity:
- Average: O(n * log(n))
- Adaptable
